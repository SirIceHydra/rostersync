15 Don'ts

  • Don't blend purple and orange into a gradient — keep them adjacent, not mixed.
  • Don't reuse the sync-circle from the logo as a generic refresh icon.
  • Don't combine more than two background tints on a single screen.
  • Don't use pure black or pure white for body text — use ink and paper.
  • Don't stack shadow + border + tint on the same element.
  • Don't use Bricolage Grotesque below 18px — switch to Inter.
